---
title: "Branding"
description: "How to set your centre's logo and brand colours in the platform."
---

The Branding settings let you give the platform your centre's look and feel. Upload your logo and set your brand colours — they appear across the authenticated app and on all generated PDFs, including your ERO Evidence Pack, Pay Parity Evidence Pack, and policy exports.

## Accessing branding settings

Go to **Settings → Branding** from the sidebar. Branding settings are only available to admins.

You can also access branding from the **Setup Checklist** in Business Settings if you want to revisit it after completing the setup wizard.

## Logo

Upload your logo in PNG, JPG, or SVG format. The recommended size is approximately 260×65 pixels — landscape format works best. The maximum file size is 2 MB.

Once uploaded, your logo appears in the sidebar header and on the cover of generated PDFs. If no logo is uploaded, the CCNZ lockup is used instead.

To replace the logo, upload a new file — it replaces the previous one immediately. To remove a custom logo and return to the CCNZ default, use the **Reset to CCNZ default** option.

<Note>
  SVG logos display correctly in the app but fall back to the CCNZ lockup in PDFs. For best results in exported documents, upload a PNG or JPG version of your logo.
</Note>

## Brand colours

Set a **primary colour** and an **accent colour** using the hex colour pickers. These are applied across the app as CSS custom properties:

- **Primary colour** — used for the sidebar header, active navigation indicators, primary buttons, and key accents
- **Accent colour** — used for highlights and secondary accents

A live preview updates as you change colours so you can see the effect before saving.

## Contrast checking

If a chosen colour may be difficult to read against its background, a warning appears inline. The warning doesn't block saving — it's a prompt to check legibility before committing. If you receive a contrast warning, try a slightly darker or lighter shade.

## Resetting to default

To remove all custom branding and return to the CCNZ theme, click **Reset to CCNZ default**. This clears your logo and colour settings. The CCNZ lockup and standard navy/gold palette are restored immediately.

## Where branding appears

- Sidebar header — logo on your primary colour band
- Primary buttons, active nav items, and key accents across the app
- All transactional emails sent from the platform (invitation, welcome, scheduling)
- PDF exports — ERO Evidence Pack, Pay Parity Evidence Pack, policy PDFs
