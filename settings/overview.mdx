Business Settings is where you manage your organisation's configuration — your business details, centre information, branding, and the setup checklist.

## What's in Settings

**Business Details** — your organisation's legal name, trading name, NZBN, contact details, and address. These appear across the platform and in generated documents.

**Your Centres** — the details for each of your centres: licence information, licensed numbers, age range, address, and pay parity configuration.

**Branding** — your logo and brand colours, applied across the authenticated app and all PDF exports.

**Review Setup Checklist** — a link back to the Setup Wizard so you can review or update anything configured during onboarding. Available from the Business Settings page at any time.

## Who can access Settings

Settings are available to admin users only. Staff without admin access don't see the Settings section in the sidebar.

## Making changes

Changes to business details, centre details, and branding take effect immediately after saving. There's no draft or approval process — updates are live as soon as you save them.
