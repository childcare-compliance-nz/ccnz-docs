---

The Your Centres page lists all the centres registered under your business. Each centre has its own settings — licence details, licensed numbers, age range, address, and pay parity configuration.

## Accessing centre details

Go to **Settings → Your Centres** from the sidebar, or click **Your Centres** in the Business section of the sidebar. Select a centre to view and edit its details.

## What you can edit per centre

**Basic details:**
- Centre name
- Licence number — your Ministry of Education licence number
- Licence type — Centre Based, Home Based, etc.
- Licensed for — maximum number of children
- Age range — minimum and maximum age in months
- Address

**Pay parity:**
- Whether the centre participates in the pay parity scheme
- Salary scale — the scale the centre is attesting to
- Scale effective from date

Save changes after updating any section.

## Multi-centre businesses

If your business operates more than one centre, each appears as a separate card on the Your Centres page. Select any centre to manage its individual settings. Staff assignments, compliance data, and module access are all tracked per centre.

If you have two or more centres, the **Multi-Site View** appears in your sidebar, giving you a portfolio-level view across all centres. See the [Multi-Site View](/business/multi-site-view) page for more.

## Adding a new centre

Adding centres is managed by the CCNZ team. If you need to add a new centre to your account, contact support at support@childcarecompliance.co.nz.
