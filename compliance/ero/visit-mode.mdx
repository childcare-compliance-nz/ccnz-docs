---
title: "Visit Mode"
description: "How to activate ERO Visit Mode to lock your compliance register during a review."
---

ERO Visit Mode is a feature designed for the day of an ERO review. When active, it locks your Compliance Register into a read-only snapshot — so nothing can be changed while the reviewer is looking at it — and stamps the visit date onto any Evidence Pack generated during the visit.

## What Visit Mode does

When Visit Mode is activated:

- A persistent amber banner appears across the entire platform for all users: **"ERO Visit Mode active — register is in read-only snapshot view"**
- All status dropdowns and edit controls in the Compliance Register are disabled — no one can change compliance statuses or notes while Visit Mode is on
- The ERO visit date is recorded and included on the cover page of any Evidence Pack generated during the visit

Visit Mode doesn't affect any other part of the platform — drills, incidents, hazards, and HR records can all still be viewed and updated as normal.

## Activating Visit Mode

From the ERO Audit Prep dashboard, click **Activate ERO Visit Mode**. You'll be asked to confirm. Once confirmed, the amber banner appears immediately across the platform for all users.

<Note>
  Only admins can activate and deactivate Visit Mode. Compliance users can see the banner and the read-only register but cannot toggle the mode.
</Note>

## Deactivating Visit Mode

Once the ERO review is complete, deactivate Visit Mode from either:

- The **ERO Audit Prep dashboard** — click **Deactivate Visit Mode**
- The **amber banner** — a deactivate button appears in the banner itself

Once deactivated, the banner disappears and the Compliance Register returns to its normal editable state. The visit start time remains on record.

## Recommended visit day workflow

1. Before the reviewer arrives — work through the Self-Review Tool, resolve outstanding actions, and generate a first Evidence Pack to check it's complete
2. When the reviewer arrives — activate Visit Mode, then generate a fresh Evidence Pack with the visit date on the cover
3. Hand the pack to the reviewer
4. After the review — deactivate Visit Mode and update your Compliance Register with any actions or findings from the review
