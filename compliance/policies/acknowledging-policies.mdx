---
title: "Acknowledging Policies"
description: "How to assign policies to staff for acknowledgement and how staff complete their acknowledgements."
---

Policy acknowledgements create a record that each staff member has read and understood a specific policy. Once a policy is published, you can assign it to any or all staff members, set an optional due date, and track who has acknowledged and who hasn't.

## Assigning a policy for acknowledgement

From the policy detail page, go to the **Acknowledgements** tab and click **Assign Staff**.

Select the staff members you want to assign the policy to and optionally set a due date. The policy will appear in each staff member's acknowledgement queue immediately.

You can assign additional staff members at any time. Existing acknowledgement records aren't affected.

## Tracking acknowledgements

The Acknowledgements tab shows every staff member assigned to the policy with their status:

- **Acknowledged** — with the date they confirmed
- **Pending** — not yet acknowledged
- **Overdue** — pending past the due date (highlighted in red)

The policy list on the main Centre Policies page also shows an acknowledgement count for each policy (e.g. "4 of 6 staff acknowledged") so you can see progress without opening each policy.

## How staff acknowledge a policy

Staff members with a pending acknowledgement will see it in their acknowledgement queue. They can access this from **Centre Policies → My Acknowledgements** in the sidebar.

From there they can:

- See all policies assigned to them that are pending acknowledgement, with the due date if one was set
- Click into a policy to read the full content
- Check **"I have read and understood this policy"** and click **Acknowledge**

Once acknowledged, the policy is removed from their pending list and the acknowledgement date is recorded.

<Note>
  Overdue acknowledgements are highlighted in red in both the staff queue and the admin Acknowledgements tab. If a policy is critical, follow up with staff directly — the platform records the status but does not send automatic reminders.
</Note>

## Re-acknowledgement after updates

If you edit a policy and save a new version, any previous acknowledgements remain on record for the old version. If you want staff to re-acknowledge the updated policy, assign them again from the Acknowledgements tab. This creates new pending acknowledgement records alongside the historical ones.
