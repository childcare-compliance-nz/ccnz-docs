---
title: "Converting to Staff"
description: "How to convert a successful candidate into a staff member once their offer is accepted."
---

When a candidate has accepted their offer and all pre-employment checks are complete, you convert them to a staff member. This creates their platform account, transfers their candidate data to their staff profile, and sends them a welcome email with instructions to set up their login.

## Before you convert

The **Convert to Staff Member** button only becomes available once the candidate's stage is set to **Offer Accepted**. To reach this stage:

1. Record the offer in the **Offer** tab — include the role, centre, start date, employment type, and method of offer
2. Set the outcome to **Accepted** — this automatically advances the stage to Offer Accepted and unlocks the convert button

Make sure the Safety Check tab is complete before converting — police vetting records and identity documents should be in place, as they carry across to the staff profile automatically.

## Running the conversion

From the candidate's Overview tab, click **Convert to Staff Member**. A modal walks you through two steps:

**Step 1 — Confirm details.** Review the pre-filled details from the candidate record and offer:

- Full name and preferred name
- Email
- Job title (from the offered role)
- Employment type
- Start date
- Centre assignment — select which centre(s) they'll work at and which is their primary centre
- Role — Staff or Admin

**Step 2 — Assign induction training.** Select an induction template to assign to the new staff member. This creates their induction training sessions automatically, ready for them to complete after they accept the invitation.

Click **Convert** to complete the process.

## What happens after conversion

- An invitation email is sent to the new staff member with a link to set up their password
- Their staff profile is created, pre-filled with details from their candidate record
- Police vetting records are linked to their staff profile
- Their induction training sessions are created and appear in their Training tab
- Once they accept the invitation and set their password, a welcome email is sent with a link to their induction training
- The candidate record is marked as **Hired** and hidden from the default candidates list

## Their staff profile

After conversion, the staff member's profile is accessible under **Staff**. Their Details, Compliance, Qualifications, Training, and Documents tabs are pre-populated from the candidate record — you won't need to re-enter their information.
