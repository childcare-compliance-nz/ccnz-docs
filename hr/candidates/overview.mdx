The Candidates section manages your recruitment pipeline. Every candidate is linked to a vacancy and moves through a series of stages from first contact to hire. The candidate record holds everything in one place — their application, interviews, reference checks, safety checks, offer details, and a full activity timeline.

## The pipeline stages

Candidates move through the following stages:

| Stage | What it means |
|-------|---------------|
| **New** | Record created, introduction email not yet sent |
| **Applied** | Candidate has submitted their application form |
| **Interviewing** | Interview process underway |
| **Reference Check** | References being verified |
| **Safety Check** | Police vetting and identity verification underway |
| **Offer Sent** | A job offer has been made |
| **Offer Accepted** | Admin has confirmed the offer is accepted — unlocks Convert to Staff |
| **Hired** | Candidate has been converted to a staff member |
| **Declined** | Candidate was unsuccessful or withdrew |

Status is shown as a visual progress bar on the candidate record. You can move a candidate to any stage at any time — stages don't lock out sections of the record, and information can be added in any order.

## The candidate record

Each candidate has a detail page with eight tabs:

**Overview** — contact details, status bar, action buttons, and internal notes.

**Application** — a read-only view of everything the candidate submitted in their application form.

**Activity** — a chronological timeline of every action on this candidate record, logged automatically. Status changes, emails sent, interviews added, notes — all captured without any extra steps.

**Interviews** — all interview records for this candidate, with questions, responses, ratings, and recommendations.

**References** — reference check records, pre-filled with referee details from the application form.

**Safety Check** — identity document verification checklist and police vetting records.

**Offer** — offer details and outcome. Setting the outcome to Accepted automatically advances the stage and unlocks the Convert to Staff Member button.

**Documents** — uploaded files attached to this candidate.

## Candidates list

The Candidates list is grouped by vacancy so you can see the pipeline for each role at a glance. Hired candidates are hidden by default — use the **Show hired** toggle to include them.

<Note>
  Candidates and HR access require either admin access or the HR access permission enabled on your user account.
</Note>
