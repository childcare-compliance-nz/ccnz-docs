---
title: "Application Form"
description: "How to send a candidate their application link and what the form collects."
---

The application form is how candidates provide their full details before being considered for a role. It's sent to candidates via a unique link — they complete it in their own time without needing a platform account, and their responses are saved automatically as they go.

## Sending the application link

When you create a candidate record, the first step is sending them their application link. From the candidate's Overview tab, click **Send Introduction Email**. This sends a branded email to the candidate with a unique link to their application form and a link to resend it if needed.

The email asks the candidate to provide as much detail as possible, explaining that a full timeline of their work, study, and immigration history is needed as part of the pre-employment risk assessment process.

## What the form collects

The application form covers the following sections:

**Personal details** — full name, preferred name, email, phone, current location.

**Teacher registration** — whether the candidate is a registered teacher, their registration number, and expiry date.

**Qualifications** — a repeating section for each qualification: name, institution, and year completed.

**First aid certificate** — whether they hold a current certificate, provider, course name, and expiry date.

**Employment history** — a repeating section for each previous role, including employer, position, dates, reason for leaving, and referee details. Referees listed here are pre-filled into the Reference Check tab when you start checking references.

**Teaching qualifications & registration** — additional fields for NZ ECE-specific qualification and service hour information (shown only if your centre participates in the pay parity scheme).

**Visa / immigration** — work rights status, visa type, and expiry date.

**Police vetting** — acknowledgement that police vetting will be required.

**Consent & declaration** — the candidate signs off on the information they've provided.

## How the form works

The form auto-saves as the candidate works through it — if they close the browser and return later, their progress is preserved. Once they submit, the form is locked to read-only and their status advances to **Applied**. All submitted data appears in the Application tab on their candidate record.

<Note>
  The application link is unique to each candidate. If a candidate loses their link, you can resend it from the Overview tab using the Resend Application Link button.
</Note>
