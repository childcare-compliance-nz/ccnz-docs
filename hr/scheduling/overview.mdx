My Schedule is a Calendly-style scheduling tool built into the HR module. Instead of going back and forth to find a time, you set your availability once and send candidates or referees a booking link. They pick a slot themselves, and both parties receive a confirmation with all the details.

## How it works

**You set your availability** — configure which days and times you're available for interviews and reference checks, and how long each slot should be. This is your availability template.

**You send a booking link** — from a candidate's record, send them (or a referee) a personalised link. The link takes them to a clean booking page showing your available slots in New Zealand time.

**They pick a slot** — the candidate or referee selects a time, enters their name and email to confirm, and the appointment is booked. Both of you receive a confirmation email with the appointment details and a calendar file to add to your calendar.

**You see it in My Schedule** — the booked appointment appears in your weekly schedule view. You can cancel or reschedule from there.

## The schedule view

My Schedule shows a week view of your upcoming appointments — interviews and reference checks — with the name, time, and type of each booking. Navigate forward and back by week, or jump back to the current week. All times are shown in New Zealand time.

## Reminders

A reminder email is sent automatically to both parties before each appointment. The reminder includes a reschedule link so the candidate or referee can change their time without needing to contact you.

<Note>
  My Schedule and the scheduling features are available to admins and users with HR access. Availability is set per user — each HR user manages their own schedule.
</Note>
