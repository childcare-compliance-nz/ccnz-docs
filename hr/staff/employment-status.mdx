---
title: "Employment Status"
description: "How to record and track a staff member's employment status over time."
---

The Employment Status section on the Details tab tracks how a staff member's employment type has changed over time. Like address history, each status change is recorded as a new entry — previous statuses are kept on record and never overwritten.

## Recording an employment status

From the staff profile Details tab, scroll to the **Employment Status** section. The current status is shown as a badge at the top.

To record a new status, select from:

- **Fixed Term Employee**
- **Permanent Employee**
- **Reliever**
- **Paid Parental Leave**
- **Unpaid Parental Leave**
- **Former Employee**

Set the **Effective From** date — this can be a past date if you're recording a status change retrospectively. Add an optional note for any relevant context.

Click **Save Employment Status**. The new status becomes current and the previous status moves into the history table.

## Employment status history

All past statuses are listed in the history table with the effective date, any notes, who recorded the change, and when. History is permanent — entries cannot be edited or deleted.

Recording employment status accurately is useful for compliance reporting and for understanding your staffing picture over time, particularly for pay parity purposes.
