---
title: "Police Vetting"
description: "How to record and track police vetting for staff members."
---

All staff who work directly with children at your centre are required to have a current police vet. The Police Vetting section on the staff profile Compliance tab holds a complete record of every vetting check for that person — including checks carried over from their candidate record if they came through the recruitment pipeline.

## Adding a police vetting record

From the staff profile **Compliance** tab, scroll to the **Police Vetting** section and click **Add Police Vetting Record**.

Fill in:

- **Vetting agency** — the name of the agency that conducted the vetting (e.g. NZ Police, or an approved vetting provider)
- **Agency type** — Internal or External
- **Form signed date** — when the staff member signed the consent form
- **Submitted date** — when the form was submitted to the agency
- **Result date** — when the result was received
- **Result** — Pending, Clear, Conditions, or Declined
- **Comments** — any relevant notes

Click **Save**. When the result is anything other than Pending, the next due date is automatically calculated as three years from the result date.

## Vetting records from recruitment

If the staff member came through the candidate pipeline, any police vetting records added during the safety check stage carry across automatically when they're converted to a staff member. You won't need to re-enter them.

## Expiry alerts

The platform tracks the next due date on each vetting record and flags staff whose vetting is approaching expiry or has lapsed:

- **Amber** — vetting due within 90 days
- **Red** — vetting overdue

These alerts appear on your main dashboard so they stay visible until the vetting is renewed. Staff who have been exempted from vetting don't appear in these alerts — see [Exemptions](/hr/staff-compliance/exemptions).

## Multiple records

A staff member can have multiple vetting records — one for each time vetting has been conducted. The most recent result is used for the compliance status badge. All past records remain on file for audit purposes.

## Result statuses

| Result | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Form submitted, result not yet received |
| **Clear** | Vetting passed with no concerns |
| **Conditions** | Passed with conditions noted |
| **Declined** | Vetting not passed |
