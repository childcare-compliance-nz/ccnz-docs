---
title: "Teacher Registration"
description: "How to record and track Teaching Council registration for staff members."
---

Certificated teachers at your centre must hold a current Teaching Council of Aotearoa New Zealand registration. The Teacher Registration section on the Compliance tab records their registration details and tracks expiry status, with a full history of every update.

## Recording teacher registration

From the staff profile **Compliance** tab, scroll to the **Teacher Registration** section.

Fill in:

- **Registered teacher** — Yes or No
- **Registration number** — their Teaching Council registration number (if registered)
- **Expiry date** — when their current registration expires

Click **Save**. A new entry is added to the registration history, and the registration status badge updates automatically.

## Registration status

The status badge is calculated from the expiry date:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🟢 **Current** | Registration is valid and not due soon |
| 🟡 **Expiring Soon** | Registration expires within 30 days |
| 🔴 **Expired** | Registration expiry date has passed |

## Registration history

Every time teacher registration details are saved, a new row is added to the Registration History table below the form. The history shows:

- Whether they were registered at the time
- Registration number
- Expiry date
- Status badge (calculated at the time of recording)
- Who recorded it and when

History is permanent — entries cannot be edited or deleted. This gives you an auditable record of registration status over time.

## Staff who aren't registered teachers

For staff who are not certificated teachers — teacher aides, administrators, cooks, and other support roles — set **Registered teacher** to No. No registration number or expiry date is needed.
