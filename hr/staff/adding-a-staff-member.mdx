---
title: "Adding a Staff Member"
description: "How to add a new staff member — with or without a platform login."
---

There are three ways a staff member can appear in your Staff list:

- **Add Staff** — create a record manually with no login
- **Invite User** — create a record and send a login invitation at the same time
- **Convert from Candidate** — when a candidate is hired, their record carries across automatically

## Adding a staff record without a login

From the Staff page, click **Add Staff**. Fill in the details:

- **Full name** (required)
- **Preferred name**
- **Email** (required — used if you invite them later)
- **Phone**
- **Job title**
- **Employment type** — Full Time, Part Time, or Casual
- **Start date**
- **Centre assignment** — which centre(s) they work at, and which is their primary centre

Click **Save** to create the record. The staff member appears in the list with a **Not invited** status. You can immediately start adding their compliance data, qualifications, and documents.

## Inviting a staff member to log in

If you want the staff member to have platform access, click **Invite Later** from their profile — or click **Invite User** from the Staff list to create a record and send an invitation in one step.

Either way, an invitation email is sent with a link for them to set a password. Once they accept, their status changes to **Active** and they can log in. All data already entered on their record is preserved.

## What staff can see when they log in

Staff with a standard login can see their own training modules, policy acknowledgements, and any induction training assigned to them. They can't see other staff records, HR data, or compliance management areas unless they have additional access permissions.

<Note>
  Staff members who don't need platform access — casual relievers, support staff — can have complete compliance records without ever being invited. Their records count towards compliance reporting the same as any other staff member.
</Note>
