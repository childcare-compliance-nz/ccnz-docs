---
title: "Training"
description: "How to view and manage a staff member's training and induction progress from their profile."
---

The Training tab on the staff profile shows a complete record of every training session associated with that person — induction modules assigned at hire, ongoing compliance training, and any other modules they've been enrolled in.

## Induction training

Induction training assigned when the staff member was converted from a candidate appears at the top of the Training tab in its own section. Each induction module shows:

- **Module name**
- **Status** — Not Started, In Progress, or Complete
- **Result** — Passed or Needs Review (once completed)
- **Date completed**

An overall induction completion percentage shows how far through the induction programme they are, and any required modules that aren't yet complete are flagged.

## Ongoing training records

Below the induction section, all other training session records are listed — compliance modules, refresher training, and any other sessions they've been enrolled in. Each record shows:

- **Module name**
- **Session date**
- **Result** — Passed, Needs Review, or in progress
- **Signed status** — whether the staff member and manager have both digitally signed off

Click any session to go to the full session detail page.

## Training assigned elsewhere

Training is managed and assigned from the Training & Compliance module rather than from the staff profile — the Training tab here is a read-only view of that person's full training history. To assign a new training session, enrol the staff member from the relevant training module.

<Note>
  If a staff member doesn't have a login yet, training sessions can still be assigned to their record. They'll appear on the Training tab and be available for the staff member to complete once they accept their invitation and log in.
</Note>
