---
title: "First Aid"
description: "How to record and track first aid certificate details for staff members."
---

The First Aid section on the staff profile Qualifications tab records whether a staff member holds a current first aid certificate, along with the provider, course, and expiry date. A full history is kept every time the details are updated.

## Recording a first aid certificate

From the staff profile **Qualifications** tab, scroll to the **First Aid Certificate** section.

Fill in:

- **Holds a current certificate** — Yes or No
- **Training provider** — the organisation that issued the certificate (e.g. St John, Red Cross)
- **Course name** — the specific course completed
- **Expiry date** — when the certificate expires

Click **Save First Aid**. A new entry is added to the First Aid History table below, and the status badge updates automatically.

## Certificate status

The status badge is calculated from the expiry date:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🟢 **Current** | Certificate is valid and not due soon |
| 🟡 **Expiring Soon** | Certificate expires within 30 days |
| 🔴 **Expired** | Certificate expiry date has passed |

Staff who have indicated they don't hold a certificate show a separate indicator.

## First aid history

Every time first aid details are saved, a new row is added to the First Aid History table. The history shows whether they held a certificate at the time, the provider, course, expiry date, status badge, who recorded it, and when.

History is permanent — entries cannot be edited or deleted. This gives you an auditable record of first aid status over time.

## Uploading a certificate

If you have a copy of the certificate to keep on file, upload it to the staff member's **Documents** tab. Attaching it to the Documents tab keeps it alongside their other compliance files and makes it easy to retrieve for ERO review.
