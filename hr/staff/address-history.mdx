---
title: "Address History"
description: "How to record and track a staff member's address over time."
---

The Address History section on the Details tab keeps a running record of a staff member's addresses. Each time their address changes, you add a new entry — previous addresses are kept on record and never overwritten.

## Recording an address

From the staff profile Details tab, scroll to the **Address History** section. The most recent address is shown at the top.

To add a new or updated address, fill in:

- **Address Line 1** (required)
- **Address Line 2** (optional)
- **Suburb**
- **City** (required)
- **Postcode**
- **Effective from** — the date the staff member moved to this address. You can enter a past date.

Click **Save Address**. The new address becomes the current address and the previous one moves into the history table below.

## Address history table

All past addresses are listed in the history table with the effective date, who recorded it, and when it was recorded. History is permanent — entries cannot be edited or deleted.

This record is useful for compliance purposes and for maintaining accurate contact information over time.
