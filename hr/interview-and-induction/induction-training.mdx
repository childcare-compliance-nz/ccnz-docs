---
title: "Induction Training"
description: "How to set up induction templates and manage induction training for new staff members."
---

Induction training is assigned automatically when a candidate is converted to a staff member. You define one or more induction templates — sets of training modules to assign to new hires — and select the appropriate template at the point of conversion. The new staff member receives a welcome email with a link to their induction training as soon as they set up their login.

## Setting up induction templates

Before you can assign induction training at hire, you need to create at least one induction template. Templates are managed from **HR → Settings → Induction Templates**.

Click **Create Template** and fill in:

- **Template name** — something descriptive (e.g. "Teacher Induction", "Support Staff Induction")
- **Description** — optional notes about who this template is for

Then add modules to the template from your published training library. For each module you select, mark it as **Required** or **Optional**. Modules can be reordered by dragging.

You can create as many templates as you need — for example, different templates for teaching staff, reliever staff, and support roles.

## Assigning induction at hire

When you convert a candidate to a staff member, the conversion modal includes an induction training step. Toggle **Assign induction training** to Yes, then select which template to assign.

On conversion:

- A self-paced training session is created for each module in the selected template
- The sessions are immediately visible on the staff member's Training tab
- Once the staff member accepts their invitation and sets their password, a welcome email is sent with a direct link to their induction training

## Tracking induction progress

Induction training progress is visible on the staff member's Training tab under the **Induction Training** section at the top. It shows:

- Each induction module with its status — Not Started, In Progress, or Complete
- The result for completed modules — Passed or Needs Review
- An overall completion percentage
- A flag if any required modules are not yet complete

## Staff without a login

If a staff member was added without a login invitation, their induction sessions can still be created manually by assigning training from the Training & Compliance module. They'll be able to access and complete their induction once they're invited and log in.
