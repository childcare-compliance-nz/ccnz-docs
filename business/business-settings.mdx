Business Settings is where you manage the core details of your organisation on Childcare Compliance NZ. Find it at the bottom of the sidebar.

## Organisation

Your business's legal and trading details:

- **Legal name** — the registered legal name of your business entity
- **Trading name** — the name your centres trade under
- **NZBN** — your New Zealand Business Number

## Contact & Address

Your business contact details and primary address. These are used across the platform where your organisation's contact information is needed.

## Branding

Your logo and brand colours are applied across the app and PDF exports. If left unset, the default Childcare Compliance NZ theme is used.

### Logo

Upload your centre or business logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG — max 2 MB, recommended 260×65px). Your logo appears in the sidebar, on PDF exports, and in transactional emails.

<Note>
  Use a logo that stays legible on a coloured background — one with its own light or solid background, or light artwork on a transparent background. Use **Send myself a test email** to check how it looks before saving.
</Note>

### Brand Colours

Set a primary and accent colour to match your centre's brand. A live preview shows how your colours will appear across the app — header, buttons, links, and navigation.

If your chosen colours may be difficult to read, the platform will flag a warning so you can adjust before saving.

Click **Reset to CCNZ default** at any time to revert to the standard Childcare Compliance NZ colour theme.

### Preview Your Branded Email

Use **Send myself a test email** to receive a sample candidate invitation email showing your logo, colours, and footer. The email goes only to your own account email address — no candidates are contacted.
