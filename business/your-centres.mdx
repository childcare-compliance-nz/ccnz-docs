Your Centres is where you manage the physical locations that make up your business. Each centre has its own licence details, contact information, and pay parity configuration.

## Centre List

The Centres page shows all centres registered under your business. Each card displays the centre name, MoE licence number, location, and licensed numbers at a glance. Click any centre to open its detail page.

To add a new centre, click **+ New centre** in the top right.

## Centre Detail

Clicking into a centre shows three sections:

### Licence

Key licensing information for the centre:

- **Licence number** — the Ministry of Education licence number
- **Licence type** — Centre-Based, Home-Based, etc.
- **Licensed numbers** — the maximum number of children licensed to attend
- **Age range** — the age range the centre is licensed for
- **Added** — when the centre was added to CCNZ

### Contact

The centre's contact details — phone, email, and physical address.

### Pay Parity

A summary of the centre's pay parity configuration:

- Whether the centre is participating in the ECE Pay Parity scheme
- The current salary scale (Full Parity or otherwise)
- The date the scale became effective
- The next RS7 attestation due date
- The status of the current attestation period
- Whether the centre is under a moratorium
- Attestation history — a record of all previous RS7 attestations for this centre

Click **View register** to go directly to the Pay Parity module for this centre.

## Managing Centres

From the centre detail page you can:

- **Edit** — update licence details, contact information, and pay parity settings
- **Assigned users** — view and manage which staff members have access to this centre
- **Close centre** — mark a centre as closed (this does not delete any data)
