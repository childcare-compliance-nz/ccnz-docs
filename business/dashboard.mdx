title: "Your Dashboard"
description: "A live snapshot of your business's compliance status across all modules."
---

The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you log in to Childcare Compliance NZ. It gives you a live view of what needs attention across your entire business — so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Summary Strip

At the top of the dashboard, three summary tiles give you an at-a-glance picture of your business:

- **Centres** — the number of centres registered under your business
- **Team members** — the total number of staff across all centres
- **Modules** — the number of compliance modules enabled for your account

These are read-only and update automatically as your data changes.

## Alert Cards

Below the summary strip, the dashboard shows a live feed of compliance alerts — one card per module. Each card surfaces the most important status information for that module and links directly through to it.

| **Card** | **What it shows** |
| --- | --- |
| Policy Acknowledgements | Policies assigned to you that are waiting to be read and acknowledged |
| Pay Parity | Educators due for their annual pay parity review |
| Compliance Register | Number of criteria that are non-compliant or under review out of 80 |
| ERO Readiness | Your current ERO readiness percentage |
| Hazard & Premises Register | Open hazards by severity (Red / Amber), items due for review, and whether a daily check has been completed today |
| Incident & Notification Hub | Open incidents, overdue notifications, and outstanding actions |
| Maintenance Log | Open maintenance requests by priority (High / Overdue / Medium) |
| Evidence Vault | Documents by status (Expired / Expiring / Current) |

Click **Open** on any card to go directly to that module.

<Note>
  Only cards for modules enabled on your account will appear on the dashboard.
</Note>

## Your Modules

Below the alert cards, the **Your Modules** section shows all the compliance modules available to your business with a brief description of each. Use this as a quick reference or shortcut to navigate directly to any module.

## My Tasks

At the bottom of the dashboard, **My Tasks** shows all outstanding actions assigned to you across every module — with the centre name, module, and due date. Overdue tasks are highlighted in red.

Tasks are created automatically by the system when actions are required — for example, when a fire evacuation deficiency is logged, or a maintenance request is raised.
