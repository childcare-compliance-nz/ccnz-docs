Childcare Compliance NZ is organised into modules — each one covering a specific area of your centre's compliance obligations. Here's what each module does and where to find it.

<Note>
Not all modules may be enabled for your account. Contact your administrator or support@childcarecompliance.co.nz if you need access to a module that isn't visible in your sidebar.
</Note>

## Your Dashboard

The dashboard is the first thing you see when you log in. It gives you a live snapshot of your centre's compliance status — open incidents, staff with expiring vetting, overdue drills, upcoming policy reviews, and more. Everything that needs your attention surfaces here so nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## Day to Day

### Incidents

Record and manage incidents involving children, staff, and visitors. Each incident moves through a five-stage workflow — Log → Assess → Assign → Resolve → Close — with prompts at each stage to capture the right information.

The Incidents module also handles **regulatory notifications**. If an incident is notifiable to the Ministry of Education, WorkSafe, Oranga Tamariki, or another body, you can record each notification directly against the incident, with due-by dates and overdue alerts on the dashboard.

**Find it:** Day to Day → Incidents

---

### Hazards & Premises

Manage the physical hazards at your centre with a standing hazard register. Each hazard has an initial and residual risk rating (Red / Amber / Green), control measures, an owner, and a review due date.

The Premises module lets you define the locations at your centre — classrooms, outdoor areas, entrances — and link hazards to specific locations. You can also set up **daily hazard check templates** so staff can complete a quick walkthrough each day and flag anything that needs attention.

**Find it:** Day to Day → Hazards & Premises

---

### Emergency & Drills

Record your emergency drills and manage your emergency preparedness documentation. The module covers:

- **Drills** — log each drill with date, type, outcome, evacuation time, and any deficiencies found. Deficiencies are tracked through to resolution.
- **Evacuation Scheme** — upload your current evacuation scheme and track its review date.
- **Emergency Plans** — manage plans for fire, earthquake, flood, lockdown, and medical emergencies.
- **Supplies** — maintain your emergency supplies checklist and record when items were last checked.

**Find it:** Day to Day → Emergency & Drills

---

### Fire Evacuation

A dedicated module for your fire evacuation records. Log each evacuation drill, track your evacuation scheme, and manage your fire evacuation refresher training for staff.

**Find it:** Day to Day → Fire Evacuation

---

### Maintenance Log

Log and track maintenance requests across your centre. Maintenance items can be linked to hazards so you can track the full chain from identified risk through to resolution.

**Find it:** Day to Day → Maintenance Log

---

## Compliance Monitoring

### Compliance Register

Your central compliance tracking tool. The register maps all 80+ April 2026 licensing criteria, lets you record your compliance status for each one, link evidence, assign owners, and set review dates.

The **ERO readiness score** at the top of the register gives you a live percentage of how many criteria you have covered — so you always know where you stand.

**Find it:** Compliance Monitoring → Compliance Register

---

### Centre Policies

Manage your centre's policy library. Adopt from 64 pre-written policy templates mapped to the April 2026 licensing criteria, or create your own policies from scratch.

Key features:
- **Adopt from template** — customise and publish a pre-written policy in minutes
- **Staff acknowledgements** — collect digital acknowledgements from staff that they have read and understood each policy
- **Review cycles** — set review dates and get alerts when policies are due for review

**Find it:** Compliance Monitoring → Centre Policies

---

### Evidence Vault

A central document store for your compliance evidence. Upload files, link them to licensing criteria in the Compliance Register, and track expiry dates. The vault alerts you when documents are approaching expiry so your evidence stays current.

**Find it:** Compliance Monitoring → Evidence Vault

---

### ERO Audit Prep

When an ERO review is approaching, enable **ERO Visit Mode**. This gives you a focused view of your compliance readiness, lets you generate an evidence pack, and helps you walk into your review with confidence.

**Find it:** Compliance Monitoring → ERO Audit Prep

---

## Human Resources

### Staff

The HR module is where you manage your team's compliance records. Each staff member has a profile with dedicated tabs for:

- **Compliance** — police vetting records, teacher registration, and visa/work rights — all with history tracking and expiry alerts
- **Qualifications** — first aid certificates and other qualifications
- **Training** — induction and refresher training records
- **Pay Parity** — qualification group, salary scale, review history, and RS7 attestation
- **Documents** — staff documents stored securely

**Find it:** Human Resources → Staff

---

### Pay Parity

The Pay Parity register gives you a business-level view of all your certificated teachers and their pay parity status. Record pay reviews, track compliance with the NZ Government's ECE Pay Parity salary scale, and generate your RS7 attestation.

**Find it:** Human Resources → Pay Parity

---

### Vacancies & Candidates

Manage your recruitment pipeline from vacancy to hire. Post vacancies, collect applications via a branded public application form, manage candidates through interview and reference checking stages, and convert successful candidates to staff members — with police vetting and compliance records carrying across automatically.

**Find it:** Human Resources → Vacancies / Candidates

---

### Interview & Induction

Manage your interview question library and induction training. Assign induction training packs to new staff and track their completion across all induction modules.

**Find it:** Human Resources → Interview & Induction

---

## Multi-Site View

If your business operates two or more centres, the **Multi-Site View** gives you a portfolio-level dashboard showing compliance status across all your centres at once. Identify which centre needs attention, compare metrics side by side, and click through directly to the relevant module for any centre.

**Find it:** Multi-Site View (top of sidebar — visible for businesses with 2+ centres)

---

## Business Settings

Manage your business profile, centre details, and user permissions from Business Settings. Invite staff, assign centre access and module permissions, and configure your branding.

**Find it:** Business Settings (bottom of sidebar)
