# Welcome

Childcare Compliance NZ (CCNZ) is a compliance platform built specifically for New Zealand early childhood centres. It helps you manage your day-to-day compliance obligations, keep staff records up to date, and be ready for your next ERO visit — without the paperwork.

## What you can do with CCNZ

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Day to Day" icon="calendar-check">
    Record incidents, log hazards, run emergency drills, and maintain your fire evacuation records.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Staff & HR" icon="users">
    Track police vetting, teacher registration, pay parity, first aid certificates, and qualifications.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Policy Library" icon="book">
    Adopt from 64 pre-written policy templates mapped to the April 2026 licensing criteria.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ERO Audit Prep" icon="clipboard-check">
    Generate an evidence pack, track your readiness score, and enable ERO Visit Mode.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who is this documentation for?

These docs are for **centre administrators and managers** using the CCNZ platform. Whether you're setting up for the first time or looking for help with a specific feature, you're in the right place.
