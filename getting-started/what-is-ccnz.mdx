Childcare Compliance NZ (CCNZ) is a compliance management platform built specifically for New Zealand early childhood centres. It replaces spreadsheets, paper records, and disconnected systems with a single place to manage everything your centre needs to stay compliant with the Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations 2008 and the April 2026 licensing criteria.

## Why it was built

ECE compliance is complex. Centre managers are responsible for tracking staff police vetting, teacher registrations, first aid certificates, pay parity obligations, incident records, hazard registers, emergency drills, fire evacuations, and 80+ licensing criteria — all at the same time, often across multiple centres.

CCNZ was built by people who understand this complexity. Every module maps directly to your real compliance obligations, not a generic checklist.

## Who uses CCNZ

CCNZ is designed for:

- **Centre managers and head teachers** — the people responsible for day-to-day compliance
- **Business owners and administrators** — operators running one or more ECE centres who need a portfolio view
- **ERO preparation leads** — anyone preparing for an external review

## Multi-site support

CCNZ is built for multi-site operators from the ground up. A single login can manage multiple centres, with per-centre compliance tracking, staff records, and a portfolio-level dashboard showing compliance status across your entire operation.

## NZ-specific

Every feature in CCNZ is built for New Zealand ECE compliance specifically:

- Pay Parity calculations follow the NZ Government's ECE Pay Parity salary scale
- The Policy Library maps to the April 2026 licensing criteria
- The Compliance Register tracks against all 80+ gazetted criteria
- Notifiable event guidance is referenced to NZ regulatory requirements

<Note>
CCNZ is not a substitute for legal or regulatory advice. Always refer to the current Education (Early Childhood Services) Regulations and licensing criteria for your compliance obligations.
</Note>
